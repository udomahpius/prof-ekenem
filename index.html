About Me

Ekanem is a seasoned Professor of Theoretical and Applied Climatology with thorough knowledge of geography, meteorology, climate sciences, geographic information system. He has extensive experiences in teaching, lecturing, research and consultancy and Committed to knowledge management, human capacity development and environmental sustainability and Community Development

Ph.D. (Climatology) - University of Ibadan, Ibadan — 1998

M.Sc. (Climatology) – University of Ilorin, Ilorin — 1986

B.Sc. (Ed) – University of Ilorin, Ilorin — 1985

N.C.E. – College of Education, Uyo — 1979

Certificate in Computer Operation – University of Port Harcourt — 1993

Certificate in Computer Programming – University of Port Harcourt — 1993
